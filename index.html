Where is Prince Electronics located?

Do you sell DC Fans and Solar Items?

Yes! We are famously known as "DC Pankhe Wale" (The DC Fan Shop). We stock high-speed 12V DC fans, solar panels, and solar-compatible cooler kits. We are the authorized dealers for many premium brands.
